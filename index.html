About the Firm

Built around one
sector - the Irish
legal profession.

O'Higgins O'Neill is a specialist web consultancy working exclusively with law firms and legal professionals in Ireland. We were set up because we saw a gap: Irish solicitors and barristers were being served by generic digital agencies with no real understanding of the legal sector.

We work on a small number of engagements at a time. That means every client gets senior attention, not a junior account manager. We understand your regulatory environment, your clients, and the specific commercial dynamics of Irish legal practice.

The digital presence of a law firm is, in effect, its first brief to the court of public opinion -and first impressions in the law are rarely revised.
